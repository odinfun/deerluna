The Odin Lunar Deer Adventure

In the near future, ODIN NASA made an incredible breakthrough in interspecies space travel. After years of secret testing, they successfully sent the first deer to the Moon.

The deer, named Luna, was specially trained for the mission.With the magic bestowed by Odin, she can jump gracefully in a low-gravity environment.

Luna's mission objectives included:

  • Testing lunar vegetation growth potential
  • Demonstrating animal adaptability in low gravity
  • Explore the Odin magic from the Moon
  • Being adorable (which she excelled at),etc

After some time on the lunar surface, Luna returned safely to Earth, where she was celebrated as a pioneer and given all the apples she could eat.
