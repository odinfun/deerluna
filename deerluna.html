The Lunar Deer Adventure

In the year 2042, NASA made an incredible breakthrough in interspecies space travel. After years of secret testing, they successfully sent the first deer to the Moon.

The deer, named Luna, was specially trained for the mission. She wore a custom-made spacesuit that allowed her to hop gracefully in the low gravity environment.

Luna's mission objectives included:

  • Testing lunar vegetation growth potential
  • Demonstrating animal adaptability in low gravity
  • Collecting data on long-term space exposure effects on mammals
  • Being adorable (which she excelled at)

After 30 days on the lunar surface, Luna returned safely to Earth, where she was celebrated as a pioneer and given all the apples she could eat.
